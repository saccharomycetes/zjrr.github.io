Detailed instructions

Annotation Procedure

  • In each example, you are given a video in a traffic scene. (see example below)
  • Carefully watch the video.
  • Then you are given 3 questions, which ask you about the initial scene, detailed movement, and the final scene of the video.
  • Follow the question to give a detailed description of the video.

Key Advices

  • Include as many relavant participants as possible. (Including the pedestrians, vehicles, traffic lights)
  • Include as many relavant environments as possible. (Including the traffic situation nearby, )
  • Include as many details of movements in the video as possible.
  • Try to use specific names. Rather than "the car", you should use "The SUV, the sedan, the tricycle(see in example).
  • Do not use pronoun. (In every sentence, you should use "the car", "the vehicle" rather than "it")
  • Do not use negative sentences. (Rather than "the car does not move", you should say "the car stays still.")
