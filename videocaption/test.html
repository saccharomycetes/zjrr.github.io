Tips

  • Include all important participants in the video. (Including the pedestrians, vehicles, and traffic lights)
  • Include as many details of movements in the video as possible.
  • Try to use specific names. Rather than "a car", you should use "an SUV, a sedan, a tricycle."
  • If the car in the first persective is involved in the traffic event and you can not recogize the car, you may use "a car".
  • Do not use pronouns. (In every sentence, you should rephrase the participants rather than "it")
  • Do not use negative sentences. (Rather than "the car does not move", you should say "the car stays still.")
